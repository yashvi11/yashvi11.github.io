    My Assets and Soft Skills

    Communicating Skills

    Communication skills are the abilities you use when giving and receiving different kinds of information. Some examples include communicating new ideas, feelings or even an update on your project. Communication skills involve listening, speaking, observing and empathising.

    Presentation skills

    Presentation skills are the skills you need in delivering effective and engaging presentations to a variety of audiences. These skills cover a variety of areas such as the structure of your presentation, the design of your slides, the tone of your voice and the body language you convey.

    Writing Skills

    Writing skills mean the writer is able to write down their thoughts/ideas/opinions/facts/stories etc in a manner which makes their thoughts clear and comprehensible to the reader. Good writing needs to have good vocabulary, correct grammar and spellings along with perfect punctuation.
